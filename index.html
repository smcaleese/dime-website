In these times, we all know that it's getting more and more difficult to make ends meet after you graduate, especially if you don't have a job lined up before hand.

That's why we're building Kareer. Kareer aims to disrupt the current recruitment process where you must go through complicated forms and repeat the same information over and over just to apply. With Kareer, all you need is one profile, and you can apply everywhere. Even better, applying to a job is as simple as swiping right. And if your potential employer swipes right back, you'll both have matched and can move on to the interview and hiring stages.

It's apparent from the graph above that poverty is closely correlated to unemployment.

At Kareer, our goal is to help people find jobs to minimize unemployment.

We want you to love your job.

1. Fill out your profile

Add your CV, experience details and college enrollment status onto your Kareer profile to start matching.

2. Match with employers

In a few days, you'll start seeing potential employers that you can swipe left or right on.

3. Score an interview

If both you and your potential employer swiped right on each other, you'll have won yourself an interview at your next dream job.

Your questions? Answered.

How is my data used?

We only show your CV and details to employers, who aren't allowed to keep it or use it outside of Kareer.

Can I deny an interview?

Of course. Even if you swipe right on an employer, you enter no legal obligation to continue the process, and likewise, neither does the employer.

What if I have more questions?

You're welcome to get in touch with our support team at 0838475633 or drop by at 1 Canal Rd., Dublin 1.

What's in it for you?

We'd never charge you to use Kareer - instead, employers pay a fee whenever they choose to move someone onto the interview process.

Is it OK if I graduated college a long time ago?

Although Kareer is aimed mainly at people in college or those who have recently graduated, anyone can use Kareer - we don't have specific requirements.
